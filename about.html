About Me

I decided to double design as my second major in my junior year of college, I took some basic design classes beforehand and found them interesting, after choosing to major in this field I found that UI/UX is a direction I like, and can also combine what I'm learning about psychology for the users to design useful applications. I still have a lot to learn about design, but I enjoy trying new things. This portfolio site includes my graphic design, UI/UX design, and some projects I created with other tool.
