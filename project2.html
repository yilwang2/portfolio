Posters

I create posters by using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. The first poster is about the typeface: Bodoni. This poster includes history, the designer's name, the publish date, and samples of Bodoni. The second poster is a cheese festival event poster, I use different kinds of cheeses as elements to attract people who love cheese to come to attend this event.

Challenge:

Use Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop to create posters that are focused and well laid out, using the grid feature, which allows you to use both images and text, to highlight important information as well as clear typography and design, so that the viewer can intuitively access the useful information.

Solution:

For the posters, I used Adobe Photoshop for the image editing and Adobe Illustrator for the overall layout. When making the posters I first designed different paper options and then chose the one I felt was the most suitable. After deciding on the design I choose the colors, the pictures, and then the fonts, Grid is a great tool for me, it helps me to arrange the text and pictures faster and make them look neat and fresh.
